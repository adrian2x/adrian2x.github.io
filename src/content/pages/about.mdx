I'm Adrian Cruz, a full-stack software engineer who likes turning fuzzy problems
into products people actually enjoy using. Most of my work lives in the Node.js
and React corner of the web, with a soft spot for fintech, developer tooling, and
the small details that make an interface feel effortless.

Over the last decade I've built things at companies large and small - from Google
and Dropbox to fintechs like Robinhood, Capital One, and Glide - usually right
along the seam where thoughtful frontend meets real backend infrastructure. These
days I'm based in the Santander area of northern Spain and work remotely.

This page doubles as my CV. If you want the short version, jump to
[say hello](#say-hello) at the bottom.

## What I work with

Node.js and TypeScript, React and Next.js, Python and Django on the backend, and
AWS to hold it all together - lately reaching for Rust too. I care about
performance budgets, build systems, and making the whole thing accessible and
pleasant on every screen.

Top skills: Next.js, TypeScript, React, Python, SQL, and web platform plumbing.
